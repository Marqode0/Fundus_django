Everything you need to know about FundUs before you sign up.

Yes! Creating a FundUs account is completely free. We charge no monthly fees or maintenance costs. Small transaction fees may apply for transfers to other banks, which are always shown clearly before you confirm.

Your funds and data are protected with bank-grade encryption, PIN authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. We never store your full account details in plain text, and all sessions are automatically secured.

Transfers between FundUs accounts are instant — 0 seconds. Transfers to other Nigerian banks (GTBank, Access, Zenith, UBA, etc.) typically complete within 30 seconds, though network conditions can occasionally affect this.

You can sign up and explore FundUs without a BVN, but certain features like high-value transfers and full account verification will require your BVN as required by CBN regulations. Adding your BVN also increases your transaction limits.

You can reset your PIN at any time by verifying your email address and going through our secure identity check. The process takes under 2 minutes and your account remains protected throughout.

Currently FundUs is optimized for personal use. Business account features — including bulk payments, invoicing, and team access — are on our roadmap and coming soon. Sign up to be notified when they launch.
